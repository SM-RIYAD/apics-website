The DataDevelop Prize HK: 2025 Student Case Competition will bring together students and young professionals from around the world. The winning team members will qualify for the fast-track interview of internship opportunities by multinational firms.

All competition team participants can get a special student price of USD 200 (original price is over USD 400) to join Procurement Success Summit (PSS).

This year, the DataDevelop Prize HK: 2025 student case competition will be combined with a few keynote speeches that will allow participants the opportunity to learn/network with eminent supply chain people from different countries.
